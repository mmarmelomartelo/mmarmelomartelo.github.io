How to make a delicious tea

While coffe it seems more intellectual, tea is more spiritual (Chinese proverb)

ingredients

  • Tea
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar/Honey (Optional)

preparation

  1. Warm the water at 100◦C for 10 min.
  2. Add your tea bag in your nicer mug
  3. Pour hot water on top of it (smell a bit at this time is always good)
  4. wait for 3-4 min
  5. Remove the tea bag and, if you want, squeeze gently the bag using a spoon
  6. Sweeten if you want, and enjoy it!
